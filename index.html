RIGID QUALITY PROCEDURES

NO DUST . NO FANNINGS . NO BROKENS . 100% ONLY WHOLE LEAF TEA.

The whole leaf allows for a degree of subtle and wonderfully complex flavors to develop. Being the highest grade of tea, they retain more antioxidants, have more natural benefits, and have better steeping value.

We value the wholesome goodness of whole leaf tea and the high quality it brings in every cup. That is why we ensure only the finest and the freshest, from the source, with minimum damage and maximum care for a tea that brims with freshness.

REAL INGREDIENTS AND NOTHING ELSE

We blend premium organic teas with only 100% real fruits, flowers, spices, herbs, and natural oil extracts - and never any artificial flavorings.


Many tea brands use artificial flavoring and fragrances to mask poor quality ingredients that create intense and unnatural flavors. Teabox uses only authentic ingredients, sourced from dense and tropical forests of India. We don't use any secret ingredients. What you see on the ingredient list is what you sip in your cup.
